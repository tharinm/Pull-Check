Terms & Condtion

Customer

  1. If the customer cancels their job on the previous day,
    • Rs.400 will be refunded to the customer.
    • Since the customer canceled the job, the employee can participate in a job in the job list again.
    • According to the number of times a customer cancels jobs, the amount of customer’s points will decrease.
  2. If job canceled before 4 hours on the relevant day, Rs.500 will not be refunded to the customer and Rs.400 will be paid to the employee
  3. Depending on the work type, the amount paid for that work varies.
  4. Points are collected according to the number of times complete the works, and after a certain amount of points are collected, you can buy products with discounts through the “Shopping Store” on our website.

Employee

  1. According to the number of times employee cancels jobs, the amount of employee’s points will decrease.
  2. If the employee has more than 5 complaints, the employee will be banned from system for a period of two weeks.
  3. Points are calculated,
    • According to the number of completed works and as the number of reviews increases.
    • As the number of complaints increases, the number of points decreases.
  4. Points are collected according to the number of times you complete the works provided by the customer, and after a certain amount of points are collected, you can buy products with discounts through the “Shopping Store” on our website
